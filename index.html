Research Paper Toolbox

Welcome to the Research Paper Toolbox

Constructing a research paper is an integral part of being a graduate student in the Information Architecture, Technical Communication, and Information Design programs at IIT.

There is no right or wrong way to put a paper together, but there are a wealth of tools available to make the process efficient and manageable. In this course, you will find an explanation of the basic steps involved in writing a research paper, as well as a selection of tools that complement certain steps.

How This Site Helps

If you are new to writing research papers, this course will guide you through each of the basic steps. Even if you are an experienced writer, you can still refresh your knowledge with one or more lessons.

To begin, choose a lesson by clicking one of the modules below.
